Our Story

Born in Portugal,
brewed in Sussex

JO.CO is a Portuguese-inspired cafe nestled inside The Forge Garage in the heart of Handcross Village. Run by Michael, we bring the warmth and flavour of Lisbon's coffee culture to the English countryside — from our signature pasteis de nata to the strongest flat whites in West Sussex.

What started as a single cafe has grown into two locations, with our newest branch in nearby Balcombe. But the heart stays the same: good coffee, honest food, and a place where the village comes together.
