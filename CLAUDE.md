## Brand Identity
- **Logo**: White "JO.CO" on black — minimal, modern
- **Primary Palette**: Black (#0a0a0a) base with multicolored accents
- **Accent Colors**: Terracotta (#c4654a), Navy (#2a4a6b), Amber (#d4a754), Sage (#6b8f71), Rose (#c47a8a)
- **Typography**: Outfit (900 for headlines, 300-600 for body) + Cormorant Garamond (serif accents)
- **Direction**: Portuguese warmth meets modern British coffee culture. Converted garage industrial-rustic character. Dark, premium, multicolored. KAFF-inspired large bold typography.

## Design Approach
- **Theme**: Dark luxury with multicolored ambient accents
- **Hero**: Scroll-driven Three.js video (plays forward on scroll down, reverses on scroll up)
- **Typography**: Oversized bold (KAFF-style), Outfit 900, clamp(3.5rem, 9vw, 8rem)
- **Layout**: Left-aligned hero, asymmetric grids, editorial spacing
- **Motion**: Scroll-reveal animations, infinite marquee, ambient color orbs
- **Scroll Speed**: 40% slower (500vh hero container)
